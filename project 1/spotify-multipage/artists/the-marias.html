The Marías

The Marías are an indie group from Los Angeles led by singer María Zardoya and drummer producer Josh Conway. Their music blends dream pop, soft funk, touches of jazz, and both Spanish and English lyrics to create a silky cinematic mood. Early EPs built a devoted audience, and the debut album CINEMA expanded their lush sound with careful production and strong visual direction. The Marías are perfect for late night listening and calm drives, with gentle grooves, airy vocals, and detailed arrangements that reward close attention.
