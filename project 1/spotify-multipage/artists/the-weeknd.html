The Weeknd

The Weeknd, born Abel Tesfaye, is a Canadian artist who turned moody R&B into a global pop sound. Early mixtapes set a dark nighttime mood that grew into major albums like Beauty Behind the Madness, Starboy, and After Hours. He blends retro synths with modern production and writes about love, excess, and the empty side of fame. Songs such as “Blinding Lights” show his gift for giant hooks while keeping a dramatic, cinematic feel. Strong visuals and story driven videos make each album era feel like its own movie world.
