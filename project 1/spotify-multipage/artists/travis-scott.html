Travis Scott

Travis Scott is a Houston artist whose music feels cinematic with huge layered production made for live shows. Albums like Rodeo and Astroworld helped shape modern trap with booming drums, swirling synths, and dramatic beat changes. Travis is also a creative director who treats tours like theme parks, and his Cactus Jack brand reaches into fashion and major collaborations with Nike and others. He is a master at building moments with big hooks, heavy drops, and surprise guests, so his songs often sound like blockbuster trailers that shake arenas.
