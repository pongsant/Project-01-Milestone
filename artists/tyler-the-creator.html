Tyler, the Creator

Tyler, the Creator began as a bold voice in the Odd Future crew and grew into a skilled producer and rapper with a love for jazz, soul, and bright pop colors. Albums like Flower Boy, IGOR, and Call Me If You Get Lost show his move from raw shock value to thoughtful songwriting, rich chords, and adventurous arrangements. He designs complete worlds across music, fashion, videos, and festivals, always with strong characters, humor, and visual style. Tyler inspires artists to be fully themselves and to treat albums like carefully designed experiences.
